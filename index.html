Shree PM Consultancy Services

Shree PM Consultancy Services is a strategic consulting and incubation firm focused on helping organizations and professionals thrive in an era of rapid technological and business transformation.
We specialize in Digital Transformation, Project-Product-Portfolio Management (PPM), PMO establishment, and capability development, enabling organizations to convert strategy into measurable business outcomes.
Our consulting approach combines structured methodologies, industry expertise, and mentorship-driven leadership development. Rather than simply recommending frameworks, we work alongside organizations to design, implement, and sustain transformation initiatives that deliver long-term value.
The firm operates through a collaborative network of around 15 experienced associates, bringing together expertise across consulting, corporate training, digital transformation, and professional mentoring.
In addition to consulting services, the firm actively mentor's students and professionals utilising AI based framework helping individuals to build industry-ready capabilities and future leaders.
